Ugali

Ugali is a traditional staple food commonly enjoyed in Kenya and other parts of East Africa. It is made from maize (corn) flour, cooked with water to form a dense, dough-like consistency. Often served as a side dish, ugali pairs well with a variety of savory dishes such as stews, vegetables, or grilled meats. It's a versatile, hearty, and satisfying component of many East African meals.
